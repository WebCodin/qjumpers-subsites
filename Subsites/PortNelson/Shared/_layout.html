    About the port

    Located at the top of New Zealand's South Island, Port Nelson is the gateway for a region that produces forestry products, fruit and wine and is a processing base for seafood.

    Port Operations Information

    The port provides modern infrastructure, teamed with flexible customer-orientated services that include stevedoring and container packing. The region's export cargoes attract a number of international shipping lines linking to global markets.
